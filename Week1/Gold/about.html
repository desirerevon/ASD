What is IREM(I REMEMBER)?

Do you have short term memory? Are you getting sweaty palms when faced with a question you can't seem to remember, but need to?! No more sweaty palms! This application will allow you to save your notes and send you a reminder up to ten times! Don't Forget the Milk!
